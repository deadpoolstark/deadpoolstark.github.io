Nmap

Nmap, alias Network mapping. Its an amazing tool that can help you scan networks for free.
Vulnerabilities (referred to as vulns) are easy to see and vizualise.
Nmap helps cyber sec professionals to look into various networks and fix their vulns.
Important tool to learn if you want to be some kind of hacker in the future.

The Delorean



This "thing" is a cult classic called a delorean dmc 12. This one is one of the best retro-futristic cars on the planet, and also this is one of the reasons why it is in the back to the future series. This car was made by and for one person, John Delorean. Everything in this car is what John Delorean wanted, from a secret compartment to hide his weed to a door that looks cool when opened. John Delorean was the founder of DMC (Delorean Motor Company) and the DMC 12 is the 12th iteration of their first car. This car is a power pack it has a V6 and can go upto like 191 mph although the speedometer says 85mph is the max.
